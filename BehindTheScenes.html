Behind The Scenes

Student Short Film

2019

At the end of my first year at BUas, we got the assignment to make a short film. I was the producer, and with a team of 5 others, we filmed our very first film. It was a great experience, and we delivered a great product.

My school organised a Short Film Festival, where all student short films were premiered. There were also categories for which your film could get nominated. Ours got nominated for the category ‘Best Short Film’, and we got 2nd place! This is an acomplishment I am very proud of!

Producer: Noortje Driessen
Director: Christa Dirksz
Camera: Yza Zweers
Editor: Liona Debast
Production Design: Lana Breman
Sound Design: Annemijn van Dongen
