About Me

When I was a kid, I loved to make silly videos with my friends. Little did I know that I actually want to make my career out of making videos now! I don’t have much experience yet, but I love producing videos. Doesn’t matter if they are short films, music videos, or something else. Just being on set makes me happy!

On this page, I will informally tell you about myself, so you can get a nice impression of who I really am, and what I like to do.

Making Music


When I was about 6 years old, I started playing the trumpet. I have been apart of 3 different orchestra’s, but I had to stop when I moved to Breda. However, that doesn’t mean I don’t make music anymore. I’ve always loved to sing, and in 2017, when I tought myself how to play the guitar, I started picking up singing more seriously too.

Music is sort of like a therapy for me. When I feel down, I just pick up my guitar, and play something. It makes me forget what I was upset about, and after playing, I always feel better.

Music has been a big part of my life. As I just mentioned, I started making music when I was only 6 years old. If, later on in my life, I could combine music with film-making, I would be living the dream.
