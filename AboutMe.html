About Me

It took me a while to really know what I'd want to become later. I've wanted to become a chef, an architect, interior designer... you name it. But one day in High School, one of my teachers came up to me and told me that I should go to an open day of this one particular study: Creative Business, at Breda University of Applied Sciences. So I did and I fell in love with this study, or just mainly the creative business industry. Since then, I have made some short films, music videos, commercials, and I have done some other projects which I showcase on this website.
I am a very passionate person. Whenever I get excited for a project, I put my heart and soul into it. But next to film making, there is one other passion of mine: making music.

Music

When I was about 6 years old, I started playing the trumpet. I have been a part of 3 different orchestra’s, but I had to stop when I moved to Breda. However, that doesn’t mean I don’t make music anymore. I’ve always loved to sing, and in 2017, when I tought myself how to play the guitar, I started picking up singing more seriously too.

Music is sort of like a therapy for me. When I feel down, I just pick up my guitar, and play something. It makes me forget what I was upset about, and after playing, I always feel better. It has been a big part of my life. As I just mentioned, I started making music when I was only 6 years old. If, later on in my life, I could combine music with film-making, I would be living the dream.
