In Your Corner

Student Short Film

2019

In November 2019, 4th year students at my school needed production assistents to help them film their short film. I am always open to new opportunities and ways to gain more experience. Therefore I was happy to help! At first I started out as the production assistent, but later they asked me to be their 1st assistent director! I think it’s safe to say they really appreciated my help, and I had a great experience on set!

Producer: Mireille van Veggel
Director: Roos Witteveen
Camera: Bavo Soer
Editor: Oana Dochita and Bavo Soer
